Quick Print Steps

  1. Turn on the printer (power button on the front). Wait until it finishes warming up.
  2. Load paper into the main tray. Make sure the guides are snug against the paper stack.
  3. From your computer, open the document and choose File ‚Üí Print. Select the office printer from the list.
  4. Check settings: pages, color/black & white, duplex (double-sided), and number of copies.
  5. If available and required, choose "Secure Print" or "Hold" and enter a release code; otherwise press Print.
  6. Go to the printer and, if using secure print, enter your release code at the printer to begin printing. Collect your pages.

Copy & Scan

  • To copy: place the document on the glass or in the feeder, select Copy, choose number of copies and quality, then Start.
  • To scan to email/folder: use the Scan function, enter the destination (email or network folder) and Start. Check with IT for folder shortcuts or templates.

Troubleshooting

  • Paper jam: follow the on-screen prompts, remove jammed paper gently from the indicated location.
  • Low toner: a toner warning appears on the printer ‚Äî leave enough toner for today and notify the office admin/IT.
  • Print jobs not showing: ensure you're connected to the correct printer and clear the print queue, then resend the job.
  • If persistent errors occur, power cycle the printer (turn off, wait 30 sec, turn on) and contact IT with the printer model and error code.

Etiquette & Security

  • Collect your prints promptly to avoid exposing sensitive information.
  • Use secure/held printing for confidential documents and release them at the device.
